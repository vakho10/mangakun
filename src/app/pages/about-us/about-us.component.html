About Us

Guys, welcome to MangaKun!

MangaKun is my passion project. It is a manga reader built for people who love immersive stories. I wanted to create more than just a regular manga viewer. Here, every panel can come alive with sounds, sound effects, background music, and interactive overlays to make reading manga fun and engaging.

The initial idea was simple: create a manga reader that can scroll through pages on mouse click. However, I realized that it would be a lot more fun if you could move through not pages, but panels. Then I realized that panels can also have sounds, sound effects, and background music. And that it would be better to hide panels and have a way to reveal them as you go. In the end, it turned into something like a video player, but with still images.
