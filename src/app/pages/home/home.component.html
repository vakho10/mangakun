Manga are organized into: Series → Volumes → Chapters → Pages → Panels.

    The overall work, e.g. One Piece.

    Chapters are the unit first released in weekly/monthly magazines (Shonen Jump, Young Jump, etc.). Numbered sequentially: Chapter 1, Chapter 2, etc. Later collected into volumes. Each chapter consists of pages.

  4. 4. Pages

    Each page is one full manga page. Sometimes double-spread pages (two pages together). Contain panels and artwork.

    The individual frames on a page. Ordered right-to-left, top-to-bottom (for Japanese manga). Contain art + speech bubbles.
